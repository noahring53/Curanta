Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the email address you provide when creating an account, and technical information such as browser type and usage patterns necessary to operate the service. If you connect your Stripe billing, we store a reference to your Stripe customer ID but never store raw payment card data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used to send account-related notifications (password resets, billing receipts). Usage data is used to improve the product. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. AI Processing

Content you submit (newsletter drafts, article URLs, brand voice samples) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy. We do not store your generated content beyond what's needed to deliver the service.

4. Data Storage

Account and newsletter data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted in the US. Your data is protected by row-level security policies so only you can access your own content.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use only technically necessary cookies (session authentication). We do not run third-party advertising trackers.

6. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing noah@getcuranta.com.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email noah@getcuranta.com.
